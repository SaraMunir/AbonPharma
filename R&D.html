R&D

Abon’s highly skilled scientific team utilizes sophisticated techniques and technologies in product development. Our scientists and chemists identify, scrutinize, and effectively use the physicochemical attributes of API, excipients, and the finished product to connect with the drug and dosage form’s pharmacokinetic parameters in developing the drug delivery system. The use of statistical design and mathematical modeling with consideration of pharmacokinetic principles in formulation development enables Abon’s scientists to develop a product with a predictable outcome that offers higher assurance in bio/clinical studies’ success. In addition, Abon’s scientists integrate quality and regulatory requirements into the development program, giving a higher guarantee of a fast-track product review, approval, and launch.
